THREE HEALTHY JUICES FOR YOU


Pomogranete Juice


Pomogranete juice may help prevent inflammation, heart disease, and cancer. So having a glass every now and then is a good choice.

Lemon Juice


Not lemonade, lemon juice. Lemon juice cleanses the liver, stimulates bile production, and helps with digestion. Lemon also brings vitamin C to the party to boost your immune system.

Kale juice


Kale juice is packed with vitamins and minerals that help with everything from bone health to regulating your bowels.


THREE HEALTHY TEAS FOR YOU


Masala Chai


The spices present in the masala chai have their individual benefits, and they work together to help the body fight infections. After a tiring day, a freshly brewed cup of masala chai can calm you down and revitalize you..
Green Tea


A cup of green tea is a better choice than a cup of joe when that afternoon slump hits. You are jonesing for a caffeine buzz, so this will do the trick, with just enough caffeine to give you that energy boost but not so much that you crash later. And you'll also get a nice side of antioxidants with each cup.
Ginger Tea


Ginger is an excellent natural remedy for all tummy troubles. It has been promoted as a way to sooth an upset stomach, relieve motion sickness and pregnancy related nausea, and ease digestion.
